NCC Air Wing Syllabus

Practice Questions

Q1: What are the four forces acting on an aircraft?

Show Answer

Lift, Weight, Thrust, and Drag.

Q2: What is the function of the rudder?

Show Answer

The rudder controls the yaw (left-right movement) of the aircraft.

Q3: Define lift.

Show Answer

Lift is the upward force that opposes the weight of an aircraft and keeps it in the air.
